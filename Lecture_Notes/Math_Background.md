* [Infinite Series](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zJljSLqUbUc)
  * Lim n-> inf Sum i = 1 to n a_i.
  * Limit exists if for all i a_i >= 0.
  * For negative elements or mixed signs, limit exists if Lim n-> inf Sum i = 1 to n |a_i| < inf.
